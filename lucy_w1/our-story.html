Our Story

A bridge between two worlds.

How a childhood in Chinatown and a chance meeting in 2024 became Lucy Compass.

Lucy Compass was created to bring authentic Chinese metaphysical wisdom into modern life. Its founder, Lucas, grew up inside Lion Trading, a traditional Chinese cultural store in San Francisco Chinatown founded by his mother.

Through years of witnessing customers seek guidance, comfort, and understanding, Lucas came to realize that these traditions were not simply cultural objects on a shelf — they were a living form of wisdom, passed hand to hand, question to answer.

In 2024, Lucas met Master ICY, a Feng Shui master and BaZi consultant from Hong Kong. This encounter transformed his understanding of Chinese metaphysics — and planted the idea that would become Lucy Compass.

Lucy means "light." Lucy Compass represents the light that helps people understand themselves, align with their path, and move forward with clarity.

  5. 2025

    Lucy Compass was founded to carry this wisdom forward — stripped of cliché, presented with the clarity and craft it has always deserved.
