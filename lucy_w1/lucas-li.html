Lucas Li

Founder & Cultural Bridge.

李

Lucas Li grew up inside Lion Trading, a traditional Chinese cultural store in San Francisco Chinatown founded by his mother. Years of watching customers seek guidance, comfort, and understanding taught him that these traditions were never simply objects on a shelf — they were a living form of wisdom, passed hand to hand, question to answer.

"Lucy means light. Lucy Compass represents the light that helps people understand themselves, align with their path, and move forward with clarity."

Background

  • Raised inside Lion Trading, a Chinese cultural institution in San Francisco Chinatown
  • Spent years bridging traditional Chinese wisdom with modern, Western life
  • Met Master ICY Chan in 2024, reshaping his understanding of Chinese metaphysics
  • Founder of Lucy Compass, carrying this wisdom forward with clarity and craft
